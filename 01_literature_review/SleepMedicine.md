# **Principles and Practice of Sleep Medicine**

**Authors:** [Meir H. Kryger, Thomas Roth, William C. Dement] | **Year:**  [2021, 7th Edition] 


**Reason for Selection:** 
Selected as the definitive, master-level text for the entire Sleep phase of this research (Block 02). Widely considered the highest authority in sleep science, this textbook provides the exhaustive physiological, neurological, and evolutionary data required to understand sleep not as a passive state, but as a complex biological operating system. It ensures our foundational understanding of sleep architecture is grounded in clinical-grade neuroscience.
