# [NEUROSCIENCE: Exploring the Brain]

**Authors:** [Mark F. Bear, Barry W. Connors, Michael A. Paradiso] | **Year:** [2006(Third Edition)] 

## Reason for Selection:
Selected as the primary authoritative source for micro-level neurobiology. While other primers provide only high-level overviews, this textbook dedicates an explicit, highly detailed section (Chapter 2) strictly to the functional mechanics of Neurons and Glia. It treats cellular biology with the rigor of circuit design, providing the exact "hardware-level" detail required to answer RQ-003 and understand the physical infrastructure that makes memory consolidation possible
